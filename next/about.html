Hi there! RTD Go is a minimalist application to retrieve light rail times for the RTD transit system located in Denver, Colorado. We've designed RTD Go to be as simple to use as possible. The alternative options being too complex, requiring too many clicks (or typing, gross), or cost $$. We mean to fill that gap!
